Learner's Academy

About Learner's Academy.

Learner's Academy, under the aegis of the Learner's Academy Society stands for excellence and quality in education for over fifteen years. Situated nearly 20 kms from Delhi near Rajiv Gandhi Educational City and Ashoka University, the campus is spread over 17 acres in a lush green environment away from pollution. The idyllic setting and sylvan surroundings of the campus creates the perfect atmosphere to mould all round personality of children. The campus is fully Air-conditioned with 24x7 Air-conditioned residential facilities for boys and girls from Class 1 onwards. It is an institution that promotes student-centric learning and aims to provide memorable schooling to each and every child. The school aims to nurture the students for the lifelong learning with a journey of self-discovery. Affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, our academy not only offers a rich tradition of academics in the field of Science and Commerce but also embraces Liberal arts and Humanities as one of its educational heritages.

Our vision and mission.

The sole vision of Learner's Academy, is not only to make the young perfect in any one of the streams, but also to open and dispose their minds as may best make them capable of any, when they shall apply themselves to it. Learner's Academy, aims to be the school, which truly believes to unsettle the minds of the young and thereafter inflame their intellects. We aim to help each child realize his potential. We wish not to tell, explain, demonstrate but inspire our students. We wish to emphasize more on the method of teaching than the content. We are truly the school with motivation and dedication. The vision of the school "Service before Self" inspires the students to be future leaders, institution builders and renowned scholars. The mission of the school is to imbibe the intellectual, moral and humanistic values amongst the students so that they can achieve their maximum potential as an individual and as a responsible citizen of the nation.
